Who We Are

Your Partners in Employee Benefits

At Darling Bell Insurance Solutions, we believe that every employer deserves access to the same level of expertise and insight that large corporations receive from their insurance carriers.

With decades of combined experience in the insurance industry, our team brings a unique perspective to employee benefits consulting. We've walked in the shoes of both carriers and employers, giving us unparalleled insight into navigating the complex world of group insurance.

Our mission is simple: to level the playing field and ensure that businesses of all sizes have the knowledge and support they need to provide exceptional benefits for their employees while managing costs effectively.
